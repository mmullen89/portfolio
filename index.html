About Me

Matt Mullen is an aspiring full stack web developer from North Carolina. He first got interested in tech at the young age of 3, after stealing away his Mom's birthday present, a Game Boy. This event spawned a love of all things technology that lives on to this day.

Matt has a degree in Software and Information Systems from UNC Charlotte and holds A+ and Network+ certifications from CompTIA. He is currently a student at Alt Academy and is studying with the hopes of getting a job as a web developer.
